Our Story

Wild Meadows Floral provides custom, high quality sola bouquets to those who want more greenery in their lives! The shop was created because its chief designer couldn't find a sola bouquet that looked a bit more natural for her own wedding; most sola bouquets feature just flowers with small amounts of filler, but what do you do when you want a more romantic, fresh-picked, wild look? For this, you need eucalyptus, ferns, white berries, and other dried stems. If, as a bride, you share our vision of wild-spirited, long lasting bouquets that appear to be fresh-picked from a meadow, you're in the right place!
